Our Donation Pool System

Every HeroPass membership contributes to our pooled fund. Each time you make a purchase at a partner business:

  • The business is entered into our monthly donation lottery
  • 5% of your purchase goes into the pooled fund
  • Winning businesses receive surprise donations (typically $500-$2000)

* Donation amounts vary based on pool size and number of members in your area.
